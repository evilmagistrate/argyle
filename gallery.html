6 Panel Sequence- How to Host a Dinner Party


Solution to prompt requesting an illustration that describes an activity using only six sequential images.

I love to cook, so I elected to demonstrate the process of hosting a succesful dinner party. First, one must must invite guests, plan the menu, and make an ingredient list. Second, ingredients are shopped for and purchased. Third, ingredients are prepped and cooked. Fourth, the table is set, appetizers are set out, drinks are chilled, and suitable music is selected. Fifth, guests begin to arrive and must be greeted. Sixth, once everyone arrives and the food is ready, dinner begins!

Gay Pizza Wedding


In 2013, news and social media outlets were abuzz with discussion of the constitutionality of religious freedom laws. A particular facet of the discussion emerged from Indiana and captured my imagination: gay pizza weddings. Gay pizza weddings, and the credence given to the argument that homosexuality is a choice and therefore, illegitimate.

This tapestry displays choice as a double-edged pizza sword, guiding one to sausage-laden sin or Hawaiian righteousness.

Wayfinding Signage for the Charles River Bike Path


The conceptualization of a cohesive signage system for the Dr. Paul Dudley White bike path along the north side of the Charles River challenged the designer to include maps, mileage, feature icons, safety rules, and a historical component.

This design addresses these stipulations with careful consideration given to legibility and functionality. The system approach to mapping the path for cyclists allows for clear and concise communication, while the non path facing sides feature a simplified area map outlining subway stations and other landmarks within a half mile radius.
