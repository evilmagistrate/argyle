Burrow Brand Identity


The branding package for Burrow (a Maine company that manufactures ecologically minded modular sleeping bag systems) began with custom lettered logotypes and a weasel icon, designed to communicate the warmth and resilience of the brand.

The identity was developed further and applied to packaging labels, playfully illustrative business cards, and fully outlined in a comprehensive brand standards manual.

Typographical Essay and Layouty


Challenged to research an influential typographer, write an article regarding their career and contributions, and present it in a manner consistent with their style was an extremely informative exercise.

Reading about Neville Brody in detail lay bare his influence upon 80s and 90s type treatments, a style thoroughly affected by the rapid development of digital technology and influenced heavily by punk philosophy.

Recycling Advocacy Poster


Modeled after photos taken while hiking in the Grand Canyon, "Eternal Legacy" interprets a dystopian future in which the beauty of nature has been replaced by plastic waste. From a distance, the landscape is evocative of the timeless style of NPA National Parks posters but upon closer examination, one finds that the landscape is littered with the silhouettes of over a hundred plastic water bottles.

One hopes that this image leads viewers to consider the amount of plastics they casually consume, and encourages them to reduce their personal impact in order to preserve nature for future generations.
